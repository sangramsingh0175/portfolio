CampusBuzz Mobile App

The CampusBuzz mobile app revolutionizes the way students stay informed about campus events. This innovative platform aggregates upcoming events from colleges nationwide, creating a centralized hub where students can effortlessly discover and register for activities that interest them. Whether it's a seminar, sports event, or social gathering, CampusBuzz ensures that students never miss out on any opportunity to engage and participate.

CampusBuzz Web Portal

Developed specifically for organizations and institutions, the CampusBuzz web portal serves as the backbone of the CampusBuzz mobile app. This comprehensive platform enables event organizers to easily create, manage, and promote their events to a wide student audience. By hosting their events on CampusBuzz, institutions can ensure maximum visibility and engagement, reaching students through a trusted app
